Ongoing Protocol
Owner Visit Request Policy
⚠️ Critical — Effective Immediately
Any owner visit or meeting request must be communicated to Pamela within 24 hours of receipt. No exceptions. This is a leadership visibility requirement — not optional.
🏠 Occupied Property
3 Weeks
Minimum lead time required to coordinate with the tenant, provide proper Florida statutory notice of entry, confirm property condition, and schedule with Megan. Never promise less than this timeline to an owner.
🔑 Vacant Property
1 Week
Minimum lead time required to confirm property readiness, coordinate access, and schedule with Megan. For properties mid-turn, additional time may be needed. Confirm condition before committing.
✓
Luz response script when an owner requests a visit
"Thank you so much for reaching out — we would love the opportunity to connect with you in person. To make sure we coordinate everything properly and honor your tenant's rights under Florida law, we ask for [3 weeks / 1 week] of lead time. I will connect with Megan right away to find a time that works. Please let me know your preferred dates and I will have options for you shortly."

Then: Immediately notify Pamela AND Megan by email with subject line: "⚑ OWNER VISIT REQUEST — [Property Address] — [Owner Name]"

Ongoing Relationship Touchpoints
Proactive Owner Care Calendar
✓
Weekly — Luz proactive outreach to any owner with open items
Every Friday, Luz reviews all open owner items in AppFolio and sends a brief update to any owner with an active maintenance issue, pending lease, or recent question — even if there is no new news. A simple "Just checking in — everything is on track" prevents anxiety and builds trust.
Weekly
✓
Quarterly — Video property walkthrough delivered to every owner
Tied to the existing inspection cycle. Inspector records a 3-5 minute narrated walkthrough video at each inspection. Delivered to the owner by Luz with a brief note. This eliminates the need for most owners to ever request an in-person visit — they can see their property from anywhere in the world.
Quarterly
✓
Monthly — Owner market update email from Pamela or Megan
A brief market update — Lake Nona rental trends, vacancy rates, any relevant legislative changes — sent from Pamela or Megan personally. Positions Verandah as the informed advisor and keeps Pamela's name consistently in owner inboxes without requiring a personal call.
Monthly
✓
Annually — Owner State of Investment Report
A branded personalized annual report for each owner showing: rent collected, maintenance completed, inspection results, market context, tenant payment history, and forward outlook. Delivered digitally by Luz with a personal cover note. This is the single most powerful retention tool — it makes Verandah feel indispensable at renewal time.
